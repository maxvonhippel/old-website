Privacy Policy
None of my apps collect any information about anyone. My apps are intended to be useful entirely offline. The only exceptions are Cutlery, which includes automatic linking to some Wikipedia pages (you can read Wikipedia's privacy policy elsewhere I'm sure, but I am not responsible for their policy), and GDORK, which is an app for using Google and thus would fall under Google's privacy policy. In both cases I do not receive any information myself. If you opt in to sharing crash information with app developers when you set up your iPhone, I may get some usage or crash metadata, but it is totally anonymous and no different from the information any other developers are automatically provided with by Apple.
