This tool is a demo of some of the functionality I developed for Kathmandu Living Labs during the Fall of 2016. This demo uses 100,000 points from user contributions to OpenStreetMap maps in Nepal. The full version, still under development by KLL, uses a complete data set with over 3,000,000 nodes, and hundreds of thousands to millions of ways and relations (counting one per version, since this tool is history-enabled). Check out the code here.

Try clicking and dragging on the activity chart below the map to zoom in on a specific period of time. Then double click again on the chart to zoom back out.
